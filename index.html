      1. In SketchUp, name your LED groups "LED" then export as .dae or .gltf and open here.
      2. Click any group or mesh in the 3D view to mark it. Click a LED-named group to mark all panels inside at once. Use Auto-detect LEDs for quick detection.
      3. Switch to 2D Mapping, load a Mapled reference image or video, then drag panels to match.
      4. Use Preview to see video mapped onto LEDs. Save your project (Ctrl+S) to resume later.
